About Us

Umu Zubeyda charitable organization established under the license given by Ethiopian Civil Society
authority to help physically disabled children
and their parents to become the best version of themselves

Our Vision

Umu Zubeyda envisions Ethiopia, in which all physically disabled children and their parents get the peace and comfort they deserve.

Our Mission

Our Mission is to improve the living conditions of physically disabled children and their parents through supporting, caring and creating secure learning environment by supporting the necessary materials and creating a working environment for their parents in the urban areas of Ethiopia

Our Values

We place communities at the centre of our development activities as we believe that only through strong communities can the urban settings help disabled, educate, promote the economic wellbeing of people and live in peace.

Our Statements

Our overall Development Goal is to improve the Living Conditions & Secure the rights of physically disabled children to give them the necessary care and help their parents to become productive in their future.

Our Vision

Umu Zubeyda envisions Ethiopia, in which all Physically disable Children and their parents get the peace and comfort they deserve.

Our Mission

Our Mission is to improve the living conditions of physically disabled children and their parents through supporting, caring and creating secure learning environment by supporting the necessary materials and creating a working environment for their parents in the urban areas of Ethiopia

Our Values

We place communities at the centre of our development activities as we believe that only through strong communities can the urban settings feed itself, appropriately exploit its natural resources, educate, promote the economic wellbeing of Addis Ababa people and live in peace.

Our Achievements

Since our establishment, Umu Zubeyda has made significant progress in supporting physically disabled children and their families in Addis Ababa.

Community Support

Successfully provided support to over 200 families with physically disabled children in Addis Ababa and surrounding areas.

Educational Programs

Established learning environments and provided educational materials for children with disabilities, helping them access quality education.

Parent Empowerment

Created working opportunities for parents of disabled children, enabling them to become financially independent and productive members of society.

Community Awareness

Raised awareness about the rights and needs of physically disabled children through community outreach programs and educational campaigns.

Partnerships

Built strong partnerships with local organizations, government agencies, and international donors to expand our impact and reach more families.
